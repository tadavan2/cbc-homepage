## Key Content Themes & Structures

### 1. **Company Mission & Positioning**

#### FNM Approach:
- **"Our Goal"**: Clear statement about purpose (breeding varieties adapted to sector needs)
- **"Our Commitment"**: Three pillars:
  - With the Producer (production, disease resistance, organoleptic quality)
  - With the Nurseries (diversifying supply sources)
  - With Shareholders (profitable, useful project)

#### Plant Sciences Genetics:
- Multiple crop focus (Strawberries, Blackberries, Raspberries, Blueberries)
- Global nursery network (USA, Europe, Australia, South Africa, Mexico)
- Research & Consulting services
- Technology Partnerships

#### **CBC Enhancement Ideas:**
- Add a clear "Our Commitment" section with three pillars
- Emphasize grower partnerships more prominently
- Highlight global reach/licensing (if applicable)
- Add "What We Stand For" or "Our Values" section

### 2. **R&D & Breeding Process Content**

#### FNM R&D Page Structure:
1. **Research Section:**
   - Characteristics of future varieties established
   - Crosses designed
   - Exhaustive evaluation process
   - Quality and shelf life improvements
   - Agronomic qualities
   - Scientific analysis (chromatography/HPLC for sugars/acids)

2. **Development & Registration:**
   - Evaluation under different productive situations
   - Collaborating farmers and shareholders
   - Registration process (Spanish Office, Community Office, European Office)
   - Varietal denomination registration

3. **Nursery Production:**
   - Licensing to shareholder nurseries
   - Conservation and multiplication of base plants
   - Control and preservation of material
   - Compliance with legislation

#### **CBC Enhancement Ideas:**
- Expand "Breeding Philosophy" section with more detail on:
  - The actual breeding process (crosses, selection criteria)
  - Field testing methodology (multi-site trials)
  - Disease screening specifics (Fusarium, Macrophomina, etc.)
  - Quality analysis methods
- Add a "Development Process" section showing the journey from breeding to commercial release
- Include information about registration and licensing
- Add "Partnerships" section (growers, nurseries, research institutions)
